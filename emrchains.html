Hospital staff are overwhelmed managing repetitive patient queries and appointment communications, contributing to 48% physician burnout rates and $150 billion annually in missed appointments, while 25 million Limited English Proficient patients face critical communication barriers that compromise care quality and safety.

üí° Our Solution

Our flagship product is Sana AI ‚Äî a smart, multilingual AI chatbot and call agent designed to support patients around the clock across hospital websites, WhatsApp, and phone calls. Sana provides advanced medical imaging assistance, supports English, Urdu, Arabic, and 25+ languages, and doesn't just answer questions. It listens to symptoms, maps them to specialists, books appointments, sends reminders, and retrieves previous prescriptions and health records ‚Äî all while speaking the patient's language, whether that's English, Urdu, Arabic, or any regional dialect.

Each interaction securely logs into structured records, giving healthcare providers actionable insights and instant access to medical history ‚Äî driving better outcomes and retention.
